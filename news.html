Introducing the Worlds 2021 Championship Ring

21 OCTOBER 2021 - BY PCS

The pinnacle moment for the eventual Worlds 2021 Championship team just got even bigger! With our global partner Mercedes-Benz, we are unveiling the design for special championship rings that will be awarded to each member of the Worlds 2021 Championship team.

These specially-designed rings, co-created by our brand and event design team and Mercedes-Benz exterior designers, will be a new keepsake to the winning team, joining the prized Summoner’s Cup during the Trophy Ceremony presented by Mercedes-Benz.

Each team member will receive a personalized championship ring designed specifically for the 2021 Champions with the year, player’s summoner name and team tricode all displayed on the custom fitted piece. The ring will reflect the iconic blue and silver color palette of the Summoner’s Cup, with distinct features and markings unique to the 2021 LoL Esports season.

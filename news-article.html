Introducing the Worlds 2021 Championship Ring

21 OCTOBER 2021 - BY PCS

The pinnacle moment for the eventual Worlds 2021 Championship team just got even bigger! With our global partner Mercedes-Benz, we are unveiling the design for special championship rings that will be awarded to each member of the Worlds 2021 Championship team.

These specially-designed rings, co-created by our brand and event design team and Mercedes-Benz exterior designers, will be a new keepsake to the winning team, joining the prized Summoner’s Cup during the Trophy Ceremony presented by Mercedes-Benz.

Each team member will receive a personalized championship ring designed specifically for the 2021 Champions with the year, player’s summoner name and team tricode all displayed on the custom fitted piece. The ring will reflect the iconic blue and silver color palette of the Summoner’s Cup, with distinct features and markings unique to the 2021 LoL Esports season.

“Championship rings are a storied tradition in sports, synonymous with the highest achievements and unmatched excellence,” said Naz Aletaha, Riot Games Global Head of League of Legends Esports. “Since the start of our partnership in 2020, Mercedes-Benz has helped elevate and celebrate the best-of-the best in LoL Esports, and we are thrilled to bring together our shared love for design and innovation to make a resounding mark on the crowning achievement of our sport.”

“The Championship Ring is an elegant and timeless masterpiece created by Mercedes-EQ and Riot Games,” says Bettina Fetzer, Head of Communications and Marketing Mercedes-Benz AG. “A ring that honors the best of the best and thus underscores our brand’s claim to leadership in the constant reinterpretation of luxury.”

Handmade by GOOD ART HLYWD in Los Angeles, each ring is created from 18 karat white gold, with a custom cut sapphire jewel and a real diamond encased in the hands of the iconic Summoner.

Prior to the 2021 World Final, Mercedes-Benz and Riot Games will provide exclusive insights into the creation process of the Championship Ring in a short form documentary that will be available on the official League of Legends Esports YouTube channel.

Make sure to tune in to lolesports.com to catch all of the Worlds 2021 action and to see who will be crowned the champions!
